About SafeRoad AI

Advanced machine learning technology to analyze and predict traffic accident severity in Dubai using weather and traffic data analysis.

Data Collection

Gathering comprehensive traffic and weather data from Dubai's official traffic accident reports and real-time weather APIs.

Model Training

Training multiple machine learning algorithms including XGBoost, LightGBM,logistic regression and Random Forest to achieve optimal prediction accuracy.

Performance Analysis

Rigorous testing and validation to ensure our models achieve industry-leading accuracy in accident severity prediction.
